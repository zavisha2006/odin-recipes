Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Today's recipe will be soft chocolate chip cookies which will just make your mouth melt. This is a simple recipe that anyone can make on their own by following the simple steps!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 2 cups of butter
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2 instant vanilla pudding package mixes
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 4 cups of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 350 degrees F
  2. Mix flour and baking soda, and cream together butter and sugar, beating in the pudding mix. Stir in vanilla and eggs.
  3. Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture and fold.
  4. Drop cookies onto cookie sheets.
  5. Bake in batches for 10-12 minutes each.
